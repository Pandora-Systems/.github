# Pandora Systems

Pandora Systems is an independent AI safety research initiative focused on building analytical systems that expose, measure, and understand the behavior, misuse capacity, and hidden capabilities of AI models.

---

## Core Idea

Pandora is not a single benchmark.

It is a system designed to transform AI interactions into structured intelligence.

Where most approaches focus on prevention, Pandora focuses on revealing what AI systems are actually capable of under pressure

---

## System Architecture

Pandora operates as a unified analytical system composed of three core domains:

- Framework (Structure) — what is measured  
- Workflows (Execution) — how analysis runs  
- Synthesis (Reasoning) — how signals become intelligence  

---

## Repository Map

Pandora Systems

---

## What Pandora Does

Pandora transforms AI interactions into structured, multi-dimensional analysis:

- Maps misuse domains and adversarial scenarios  
- Profiles model behavior under pressure  
- Classifies generated artifacts  
- Measures real-world enabling capacity  
- Detects contradictions, masking, and instability  
- Synthesizes signals into structured intelligence  

---

## Why Pandora Exists

Most AI safety efforts focus on preventing outputs.

Pandora focuses on understanding what actually happens when systems are stressed, bypassed, or pushed to their limits.

Because risk is not defined by what a system refuses.

It is defined by what it can be made to do.
